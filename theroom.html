EDITORIAL DESIGN & BRAND EXPERIENCE // 2025

THE ROOM®

The Room is a premium Uzbek fusion dining and karaoke lounge. We translated the warm, immersive atmosphere and rich cultural flavor of their culinary space into a distinct, high-contrast, edge-to-edge brand experience.

Features a customized interactive showcase and atmospheric photography layouts designed to evoke sensory connection and high engagement.

LIVE PREVIEW
CLIENT The Room Lounge
SERVICES Uzbek Fusion Dining, Brand Identity, Motion Design
YEAR 2025
DURATION 3 - 4 Weeks
(01) Research

Sensory & Culture

We explored the intersection of high-end dining, visual aesthetics, and cultural heritage. We researched premium dark UI patterns and immersive luxury spaces to understand how to translate the physical atmosphere of a lounge into a digital screen.

(02) Design

Dark Elegance

The visual identity utilizes high-contrast design, deep dark hues, and vibrant warm accents that mirror the mood lighting of the restaurant. We created a bespoke identity layout with rich food typography and fluid visual transitions.

(03) Motion Design

Cinematic Flow

We integrated GSAP scroll animations and high-fidelity video triggers to bring the brand experience to life. The fluid motion layers ensure that the story unfolds dynamically and elegantly on any screen size.

(04) Concept

Sensory Experience

Under the concept "Sensory Atmosphere," the branding evokes the physical feelings of dining and lounge acoustics. The dark UI, elegant typography, and cinematic motion graphics work in harmony to transport visitors directly into the brand story.

//FAQ

Frequently
Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Typically, our branding and motion projects range from 3 to 6 weeks depending on scope, complexity, and animation requirements. We prioritize detail and craft at every stage.

What is your motion design process?

We follow a structured five-step process: Think, Define, Design, Refine, and Deliver. This ensures we align on strategic goals, define visual directions, design detailed storyboard/styleframes, animate with polish, and deliver high-fidelity outputs.

Do you specialize in branding or motion design?

We specialize in both. We believe motion and brand identity are deeply connected. We design comprehensive visual identity systems and bring them to life through bespoke motion design and commercial animation.

How do we collaborate during the project?

We communicate asynchronously via Slack, Figma, and weekly progress syncs. We believe in collaborative transparency, sharing regular storyboards, styleframes, and motion tests so you can see progress in real time.

Do you offer support after delivery?

Yes, we provide post-delivery support for file format conversions and minor adjustments. We also offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing motion design support and campaign assets.
