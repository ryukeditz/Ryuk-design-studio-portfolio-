## Services Offered

- **Motion Design & Animation**: High-end advertising videos, product walkthroughs, spec ads, and social media reels.
- **Web Design & Development**: Premium, highly interactive portfolio and marketing websites (utilizing custom WebGL, GSAP animations, and tailored CSS layouts).
- **Brand Identity & Graphic Design**: Logos, color systems, typography styling, and visual asset packages.

## Budget & Turnaround Time

- **Standard Projects**: Budgets generally start from **$1,500 – $2,000**.
- **Services Included**: Detailed content research, copywriting, scripting, storyboarding, design, premium animation, revision rounds, and final delivery.
- **Turnaround Time**: Standard project timelines range between **3 to 6 weeks** from start to finish, depending on project complexity.
