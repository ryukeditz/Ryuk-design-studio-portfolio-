• (03) Our Process

A Thoughtful Process Behind Our Work

Built through understanding and thoughtful collaboration.
(01)

Think

Every project starts with understanding. We listen, ask questions, and grasp the business, product, and people. Clear context leads to better decisions.

(02)

Define

Ideas take form. We explore concepts, craft experiences, and build thoughtful solutions. Good design connects function with feeling.

(03)

Design

Concepts become reality. We build visual systems, refine interface elements, and craft interactive prototypes. Every detail serves a purpose, balancing aesthetic with utility.

(04)

Refine

Details make the difference. We test, iterate, and improve continuously. Each adjustment brings us closer to excellence.

(05)

Deliver

Execution brings it all together. We launch with confidence, ensuring quality and impact. The result is work that performs and lasts.

Common Questions

Most projects start from $1,500 to $2,000, which is where clients generally start. This includes content research, scripting, storyboarding, animation, revisions, and final delivery. Every project is individually quoted depending on its complexity and timeline.

Our standard turnaround is 3 to 6 weeks from start to finish, covering research, scripting, storyboarding, design, animation, revisions, and final delivery. Timelines may vary depending on project complexity, scope, and feedback rounds.

3 free revision rounds are included with every project for your complete satisfaction. Additional rounds beyond that are available at a fair extra charge, depending on complexity.

Payment is split into two milestones: 50% upfront to kick off the project, and the remaining 50% upon completion before final file delivery.
