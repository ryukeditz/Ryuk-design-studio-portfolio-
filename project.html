OVERVIEW

Viper approached us to build a complete visual identity system from the ground up. The challenge was to create a brand that felt simultaneously premium and street-ready — bridging the gap between haute couture and streetwear culture.

We developed a comprehensive system including logotype, typography hierarchy, color palette, and motion guidelines — designed to scale across digital and physical touchpoints.
